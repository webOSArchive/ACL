With OpenMobile ACL for webOS on your TouchPad you now have access to the OpenMobile AppMall. The AppMall is your one-stop shop for Android apps to download onto this ACL-enabled TouchPad! The AppMall is the largest independent app superstore in the world!

Find the OpenMobileAppMall icon in the Launcher and tap to start finding and downloading all the apps you have been waiting for! Launch the AppMall on your TouchPad (BTW it is running thanks to ACL because it is a native Android app after all!) and take a look around to figure out what app you're going to download first!

Downloading apps from the AppMall onto your ACL-enabled TouchPad is as simple as could be. Find the app you want and download directly to your device! The entire AppMall app catalog has been extensively tested and validated by the OpenMobile team, ensuring that each and every app will work seamlessly on your ACL-enabled TouchPad!

Launch the OpenMobile AppMall from your desktop!

The OpenMobile AppMall's app catalog is growing every day so if there is an app that you do not find, but would like to see included (and tested!), please be sure to check back soon!
